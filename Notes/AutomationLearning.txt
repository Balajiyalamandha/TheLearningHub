## A suggestion based on your goal

From our earlier conversations, I know you're preparing for **automation testing interviews over the next 60 days**. I recommend treating this like a structured course rather than a collection of random programs.

For every topic, create:

* **One folder**
* **One concept note** (in a README or comments)
* **3–5 Java practice programs**
* **1 Selenium example** showing where the concept is used
* **A few interview questions** with answers

For example, after learning loops, your repository will contain:

* `ForLoopExample.java`
* `WhileLoopExample.java`
* `LoopAssignment.java`
* `PrintAllLinks.java` (Selenium using a loop)
* `Loops_Interview_Questions.md`

By the end of 60 days, you'll have both a strong learning record and a GitHub portfolio that demonstrates your Java and Selenium skills to interviewers.
